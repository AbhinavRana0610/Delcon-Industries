About Delcon Industries

50+ Years of Excellence

Our Journey

Established in the early 1970s as I.D. STEELS PVT. LTD., we began as a leading trading firm in Loha Mandi, Naraina, New Delhi, specializing in Iron & Steel Products including Cold Rolled Steel, Hot Rolled Steel, Galvanized Sheets, and Color Coated Sheets & Coils.

In the year 2000, we expanded and diversified into manufacturing and metal fabrications. Today, we are proud manufacturers of high-quality Sheet Metal Press Components, Deep Drawn Metal Components, Automotive Stamping Parts, and Locomotive Stamping Parts.
